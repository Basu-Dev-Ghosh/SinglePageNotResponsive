I.C.V.P

The Ishwar Chandra Vidyasagar Polytechnic started with the aim to commence a knowldege centre commited to excellence in learning & teaching to the tribal & unemployed youth of the region. The mission of the college is to assist the students to bring forth the maturity and wisdom, self knowledge, intellectual honesty, clarity of thoughts, depth of knowledge, an independent capacity, mental courage, self discipline & tolerance to prepare as successful citizen.
